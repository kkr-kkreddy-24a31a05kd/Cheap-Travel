# Cheap-Travel
Cheap travel means exploring places while spending as little money as possible. It involves choosing budget options like trains or buses instead of flights, staying in hostels or low-cost hotels, eating local food, and avoiding peak travel seasons. With smart planning and simple choices, you can enjoy trips without spending 
💸 Smart Ways to Travel Cheap
✈️ 1. Book transport wisely
Book flights 4–8 weeks in advance for best prices
Travel on Tuesday, Wednesday, or Saturday (cheapest days)
Use trains (sleeper class) or buses—they’re much cheaper than flights

👉 Tip: Overnight trains save hotel cost + travel cost together

🏨 2. Stay cheap
Hostels: ₹300–₹800/night
Budget hotels: ₹600–₹1500/night
Homestays: affordable + local experience

👉 Even free stays possible via volunteering (Workaway, etc.)

🍛 3. Eat like locals
Street food: ₹10–₹50
Thali meals: ₹80–₹200

👉 Avoid tourist restaurants—they’re expensive

🚌 4. Use local transport
Autos, buses, shared rides = cheapest
Apps like Ola/Uber for fair pricing
📅 5. Travel at the right time
Avoid peak season (Oct–Mar)
Travel in off-season or monsoon for cheaper prices
🎯 6. Save more with simple tricks
Use incognito mode when booking flights
Compare prices on multiple apps
Pack light to avoid extra baggage fees
